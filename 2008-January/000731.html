Generally, I think you'll want a reliable network between the Grid Servers
and your database.    Therefore, I'd suggest that your user server and your
database be within the same local area network.  If you've got the servers
colocated.  It's time to invest in a local area switch.  Preferrably a
Gigabyte switch :D.
