Sorry, fractured thoughts today...
These literal URLs are only used for profiles. The authority and the  
user_id in these URLs are the important pieces of information, and  
they are used for many other things, such as sending IM, sending  
inventory, etc.
It would be really nice to have consistent REST-style handlers for  
doing these things... These handlers don't exist yet...
