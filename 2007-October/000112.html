1) grab revision 1407 off libsecondlife
2) compile it
3) copy libsecondlife.dll into bin of open secondlife
4) compile libopenjpeg as per the instructions on the wiki
5) copy libopenjpeg and friends into the bin directory of secondlife
6) apply the patch (based on r 2039 of opensim)
