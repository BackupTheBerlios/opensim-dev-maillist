Dear Jani:

We would welcome patches to OpenSim of your work. The key is that the patches:

1) Apply without error.
2) Compile without error on Windows and Linux.
3) No side affects that break other functionality are reported shortly after the svn commit.

It generally helps that those that supply patches participate in overseeing the testing process for a day or so on the #opensim irc channel as thousands of sims are updated and deployed.

Charles
