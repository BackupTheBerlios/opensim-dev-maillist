for those of you interested in the voice stuff (no, no SLVoice 
replacement yet), starting with r1758 libsecondlife's TestClient how has 
two commands to retrieve voice info

    * voiceaccount --- retrieves the voice account details for your avatar
    * voiceparcel --- retrieves the parcel voice info of the
      parcel/land/region your avatar is currently on

works with both LL grid and OpenSim.

i've done a short write-up at 
http://xyzzyxyzzy.net/2008/04/22/testing-opensim-and-secondlife-voice-settings/
