We do a similar thing with www.reactiongrid.com where we have laid out tons
of waterways with water sims on our grid. This way we assure sailors ( I
recently lived on a 1939 45 ft schooner for 7 years) they can traverse the
entire grid as smoothly as possible. We offer financially challenged users
to manage these water sims by providing boat landings and places to relax
and dock along the way. See this in our southern sims especially
http://www.reactiongrid.com/gridworx/map/map.aspx . 
 
All the water sims and over 95% of our regular sims are all on the same
provider in the same IP range even (so we can do on –demand power for events
with Hyper-V) so we assure smooth crossings. Home/Office sims on lower speed
are placed in spots where they likely will not disturb the flow of our high
speed sims.
