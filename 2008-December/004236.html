I agree as well and James has a good point in regard to permissions.  
 From a non-developer point of view (high to end user level), I've been 
waiting with baited breath for group permissions.  Right now the only 
way to allow team or group building is to disable the permissions module 
and run nightly backups while hoping nobody comes along in the night and 
destroys what you've been working on.  In my situation the ability to 
assign the "edit my objects" permission to those on my contact list as 
well allow group build permissions at the sim level would truly be a 
great help, a godsend even. 

Thanks and good work gang! :)
