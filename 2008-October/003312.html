Just finished testing this myself. Worked like a charm. I teleported from UCI Welcome on OSGrid to the ucigrid sim in only a few seconds, even faster then some teleports between sims on the same grid. Once I was there I could walk around correctly, build, move prims and change their properties, make and save a script in a prim which worked and displayed effects correctly, use the chat (tested locally but it worked), even terraform. Inventory was indeed unavailable as it was normal and my profile was also empty until I returned which is also explainable. I for one can report this as a very successful experiment and have absolutely no issues to report from what I tried. Wasn't expecting it to work so fast, well and satble :)

> There is a *very extremely experimental* hypergrid link between OSGrid 
> and the UCIGrid and vice-versa. Anyone with an OSGrid account can travel 
> back & forth. Come as Ruthed as possible, because prim crossing is still 
> not supported. The link will last until one of the regions crashes.
> 
> 1 - Login to OSGrid, into a region called "UCI Welcome".
> 2 - On that region, pull up the map and search for ucigrid. The UCIGrid 
> Gateway (the hyperlink) will only be visible on this region.
> 3 - Teleport there. This may be slow, be patient.
> 4 - Once there, leave a message on a prim! Don't try to TP to the other 
> regions on the UCIGrid, unless you want to crash.
> 5 - To get back to OSGrid, pull up the map and locate "OSGrid Gateway". 
> Teleport. You're back, hopefully in one piece!
