Sounds good, SM, but it may not be easy to figure out what to implement!

Groups in SL seem to serve two very different kinds of purposes:
social networking and access control.  

There's also a range of security levels from insecure and uncontrolled
all the way to fully cryptographically secure.

A good secure-groups mechanism seems like such a good building block
that I wrote up a wiki page about how to use them for ALL open grid trust.
I went a little overboard, but here it is.
http://wiki.secondlife.com/wiki/User:Latha_Serevi/OGP_trust_proposal_based_on_secure_groups

On the other hand, if we refuse to send group IM's to entities who don't use
cryptographic certificates, we have shot interoperability in the foot.

I don't want to muddy the waters!   But please be aware that this area 
is not
conceptually simple, and that one size may not fit all.
