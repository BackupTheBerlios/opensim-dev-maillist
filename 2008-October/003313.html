> > Hi,

> > There is a *very extremely experimental* hypergrid link between OSGrid
> > and the UCIGrid and vice-versa. Anyone with an OSGrid account can travel
> > back & forth. Come as Ruthed as possible, because prim crossing is still
> > not supported. The link will last until one of the regions crashes.
> >
> > 1 - Login to OSGrid, into a region called "UCI Welcome".
> > 2 - On that region, pull up the map and search for ucigrid. The UCIGrid
> > Gateway (the hyperlink) will only be visible on this region.
> > 3 - Teleport there. This may be slow, be patient.
> > 4 - Once there, leave a message on a prim! Don't try to TP to the other
> > regions on the UCIGrid, unless you want to crash.
> > 5 - To get back to OSGrid, pull up the map and locate "OSGrid Gateway".
> > Teleport. You're back, hopefully in one piece!
