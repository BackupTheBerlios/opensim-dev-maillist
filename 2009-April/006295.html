yes you will becasue with texture changer, unless your referencing UUIDS
that are already uploaded, IE webloading images into your sim, even if its
the same Image, everytime its fetched remotely it will be given a new UUID
and decoded... so as you can see if your displaying the same few images over
and over again, its probably just better to upload them into the asset
database instead of fetching from the web.
