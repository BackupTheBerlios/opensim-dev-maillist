Resulting from several test i did over time, i would believe most oft he
"memory leak" dicussed are caching.

There are some, sure.   And there are version with bigger ones, sure.

But running on trunk since October on a nice amount of region - there is no
big problem.

I would even like to see 48h or 72h hour cache - since people are not every
day online.

I have plenty ram free on my server.  Others may be short on resources, so
making it configurable in opensim.ini would be great - even for testing.


Besides that, my experience is that people often talk about high ram usage -
but don´t calculate their needs bases on prim, regions, scripts etc.  as
well, often people think prim is prim - wich is not my experience. The
larger, the more complex the prim is - the more ram. 

they assume, that the usage ~10 minutes after start is what should be
"regular usage" and all above is a memory leak.
