I'd like to reinforce the message that the Currency module currently 
being distributed with OpenSim is unsafe and unfit to be used for 
financial transactions. Do not do that, under any circumstances. The 
module is there simply as an illustration of the mechanisms for 
exercising the viewer's currency features. Anyone wanting to deploy 
currency in their grids should consult with software, financial, and 
legal experts.
