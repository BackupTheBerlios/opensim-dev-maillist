Some of the common questions that come up but are not easily answered on the wiki are:

1. What is the current recommended version?

2. My OpenSim.ini does not work after an upgrade with SVN, how can I fix this?

3. How do I setup a region?

4. How do I setup a grid?

5. What does HyperGrid mean?

As we move forward, perhaps making sure these and other similar questions have at least two pages referencing them might help us.

One of those pages could almost always be the FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions), which is in itself need of a little attention.

Charles

p.s. I also suspect the ongoing asset/inventory/server significant re-write going on right now needs some extra special attention as I see that one as a support nightmare.
