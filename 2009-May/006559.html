> > Would 8am PST work for everyone?
Not to confuse anyone, but just to clarify... California is not currently in
Standard Time right now.  I believe you actually meant to say 8am PDT if you
are talking about the California/Los Angeles/San Francisco area.

Which would mean a 2 hour time difference for me (I'm in Chicago - CDT).

Indiana, Arizona and Hawaii are still using CST, MST, and HST respectively.
 They don't switch or observe daylight savings time.

So I believe you mean PDT, not PST.  (As California currently observes
Daylight Savings Time)

Not all countries (or states) observe Daylight Savings Time.  For those in
different countries, you can click on this link below, and just select your
home country and it will automatically calculate the time based on your time
zone and the current "Daylight Savings Time" rules in your particular host
country:

http://www.worldtimeserver.com/convert_time_in_US-CA.aspx?y=2009&mo=5&d=20&h=8&mn=0
