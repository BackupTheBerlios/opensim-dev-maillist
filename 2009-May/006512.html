As for your other question, we have all kinds of fronts going on at the
same time. When we have major changes coming, we will always warn and
ask for input on the opensim-dev mailing list. Other then that, feel
free to contribute wherever you wish. If it's not such a big thing,
unless you're very unlucky to start at the same time as someone else,
you shouldn't conflict. If it's a big thing and not announced on -dev
channel, also feel free to start on it and do publicize it on the
channel so no one wastes work :)

We don't have any very longs plans, it's more of a fast find and develop
programming scheme.

If you don't know where to start, Mantis is always good for showing you
common places of issues that we could use help in. Also, by fixing bugs
you also see things that could be made faster or more clean. 

And if none of that helps, feel free to ping other developers and ask
what they're doing, we (some of us) don't bite ;)
