Yeah, I agree with Mike on that.  With e-mails at least we can have an
interactive discussion without "missing out" on anything.
Listening to a recorded MP3 (after the meeting is over) is like casting your
vote after the election has already been lost.  Not much fun...

the best way to foster communication (across all time zones) and get the
greatest amount of feedback.
