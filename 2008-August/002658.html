I'm sure they have. No physics, No meshing. No Scripts. One region per 
sim. No grid. Local viewer. Fast client machine.

Pick any number of the above and your possible prim count will increase.

I am going by the following:

Long distance to server (datacenter in another country)
Average laptop client
ODE physics
Meshmerizer
Scripting enabled and a resonable amount of scripts running
One region per simulator
Grid mode
8 Neighbours

In that configuration, 40K prims are not doable.
