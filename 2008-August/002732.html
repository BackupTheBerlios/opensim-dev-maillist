I was having similar issues. In my regions table in the mysql database,
there was a region with a zero UUID. I removed everything from the regions
table and then removed everything from the terrain table. Obviously I had to
reload the terrain from the raw heightmap files. The regions table gets
rebuilt on startup anyway. I'd check both tables for "suspicious" entries
(and, as always, backup everything before you start removing tuples from a
table).
