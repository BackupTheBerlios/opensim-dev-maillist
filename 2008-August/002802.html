Incidentally, that was the aim of the SOG, to push object changes to its parts, but it just didn't pan out.
 
Also, it should be noted that, at the time we were thinking of OpenSim as an 'API', not a "Server" - I don't know if people today are even subclassing and specializing SOPs for themselves today - seems everyone is taking the "attaching behaviours to generic objects" route.
 
So maybe a lot of our initial presuppositions on how OpenSim would be used has been turned moot. I have no problem with that. :-D
