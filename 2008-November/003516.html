Dear Todd:

I'm going to weigh in here and suggest that it would help all of us if one or more members of OpenLifeGrid could participate in the OpenSim community a bit more. As you know, our community communicates on this mailing list, the opensim-users mailing list, the FreeNode #opensim channel and the FreeNode #opensim-dev channel.

I suspect that many of the feelings expressed recently about OpenLifeGrid have to do with the real or perceived lack of participation in our community and I would urge you and others at OpenLifeGrid to help rectify that perception by participating. After all, we all become stronger by our community participation, so this is of some importance to all of us.

Charles
