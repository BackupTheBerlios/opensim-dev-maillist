In theory, I'm +1 on this being core.

I realise we at some stage do need to decide on things being core / not core - I cant justify withholding this on the basis that we have plenty of less deserving things in core right now, and the hypergrid extensions are something I would personally appreciate having the regular testing and checking that goes on core code.

The only potentially criticism may be code quality - I haven't evaluated this (so I'm not saying anything negative - this is in principle), but things going into core should hold a higher standard than extensions, so a patch of this magnitude should probably be evaluated by someone on that basis.
