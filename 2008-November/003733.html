Well, it depends a little bit on your projects and their timeframes.

There's quite a few people putting professional projects ontop of OpenSim right now (Shenlei's project is probably the most visible of these). But anyone doing so needs to be prepared to spend some time stabilising and hardening OpenSim, and also be prepared to encounter bugs.

If you need a SL clone, and you need it today - I'd probably send you to Linden Lab. If you have something else though that really needs an application platform, and your prepared to spend some time developing it - then OpenSim can be a perfect partner.
