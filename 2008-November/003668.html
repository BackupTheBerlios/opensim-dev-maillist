IPv6 is possible in OpenSim.

There is nothing particularly hard-coded to IPv4 - all our internal use of IPAddress for instance natively supports IPv6 addresses.

However.

The second life viewer is very IPv4 coded, including large sections of the protocol - such as the simulatorenable/disable methods, etc.

Perhaps when we have some alternate clients in - we can look at enabling IPv6 in the clients that will support it.
