Dear Devs,

Once again, thank you!

Revision 0.7.4 is superb.  I've now got my standalone-hypergrid AKA
Serverless Grid running on a dual-Xeon, 48GB, 64-bit XP-SP2 box, with 16 OS
instances, 256 regions, 2.5M prims, and virtual agents under libOMV 0.9.1
coming in on a LAN running with very few changes in the configs from the
0.7.3.1s.  The old Xeons are getting "long in the tooth" but your newest
OpenSim just keeps 'em idling along at about <5% CPU BW and <13GB committed.

As always, I'm your collective debt.  My computational social science
research would not be possible without you guys and gals.

Most respectfully,

Chris
