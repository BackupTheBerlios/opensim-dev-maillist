2012/1/21 Fleep Tuque <fleep513 at gmail.com>

> I wasn't very involved with Opensim at the time of the Master Avatar and I
> can understand why that concept was removed in favor of privilege levels
> and think that's a good change.  But having to set the Estate and Estate
> Owner by hand for each region is not a pleasant task and I also wish it
> were configurable in the region.ini file.
>
> As a use case example from the University of Cincinnati grid, I have a
> class with 20 students, each student will be assigned their own region for
> a large scale project.  When setting up their regions, even with copying
> and pasting, it was quite tedious to have to enter the estate and estate
> owner 20 times.  Because of the order in which you have to enter the data
> in the console, it's not a simple copy pastex20 as it would have been in an
> .ini file.
>
> I always feel badly that I'm not a developer myself and can't help
> contribute in that way, but in terms of feedback, I'd love to see an .ini
> way to configure ALL of the required initial region creation settings.
>  Perhaps down the line even non-required things like initial land height,
> terrain textures, etc.  It's far more workflow friendly and less prone to
> making a mistake entering that information in an .ini file than it is to
> enter it over and over and over in the console window.
>
> Having said that, though, I'm assuming there's something funky about the
> Estate and Estate Owner that makes it non-trivial to change since so many
> people have asked how to change estate/estate owner on the listserv before
> and the answer seems to require fiddling with the database directly.  I'm
> not sure why that is though.
