> 
> Please note also, that breaking the IM Service completely out of the
> simulator would leave OpenSimulator incompatible with the SecondLife
> client.  Therefore, the simulator has to touch the IM service. (It also

I think this looks generally good, but something occured to me this
morning.  I think we need to think about linking profiles to friends as
well, otherwise we end up with another really hard consistency problem
there.  

This would also make it easier to do things like in world address book
that stephan was kicking around.  As you just build another Buddy/IM
module that pulls in profiles from some sort of online address book.
