Setting aside the fact that, in this particular case, the issue extends 
beyond openSIm itself and embraces the client too. It seems to me that 
message we should all be getting from this chain of notes is that we need 
to make sure we're architecting openSim in a way that allows people to 
experiment with ideas like this. The ability to explore interesting 
variations upon a theme, without having to re-implement the entire theme, 
is what open source is all about from a technologist's perspective.

At least, that's what I think. So I ask the question, are we architecting 
it in that way? And if not, what do we need to do?
