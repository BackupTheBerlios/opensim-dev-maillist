I previous positions, I have worked with trademarks. My recollection is that a trademark applies to the visual representation of the item trademarked – which why it is different from a copyright; which is also why a Mark Drawing is required for the trademark application.

 

Hence, LL can trademark the logo ‘Grid’, they cannot trademark the word.  LL would not be able to make a claim against OSGrid or CentralGrid unless they used the identical logo as LL’s ‘Grid’.
