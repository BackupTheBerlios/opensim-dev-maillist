On Fri, Mar 07, 2008 at 02:18:58PM +0000, Michael Wright wrote:
> The problem is those patches getting lost in all the other traffic on this list. 
> 
> One thing that mantis does offer is that you can see which patches
> have not been applied (as long as someone closes the issue after the
> patch has been applied). 

The way I've handled this internally is that we have an opensim-ibm
list.  People send patches to it, once justin or I apply them we respond
with.

+1 applied (optionally the svn rev number it went in on).

It's worked quite well for us, and no patch that's hit that list has
gotten lost.
