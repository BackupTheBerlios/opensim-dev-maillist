Actually, no cyberpun(k) intended; but 'Matrix' is a descriptive term for this (legacy and rather unimaginative) arrangement of nodes. Quite possibly the bland term 'table' would work as well. 'Lattice' is probably what we want, what with groups of groups and all. (A lattice can be formed by sub-lattices)

Or, why don't we just leave the 2d-flat earth notion of a grid/matrix/table/lattice and move our mindsets more towards a 'Net', 'Whorl', 'Dispersion', 'Maelstrom' or 'Interjectory'?
