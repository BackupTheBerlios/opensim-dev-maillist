Greetings

I've created some diagrams for review with regards to an IM Service/Presence
service flow.

View the diagrams :  http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Proposed_IM_Flow

These were created from the standpoint of Presence and One2One IM.

Please note also, that breaking the IM Service completely out of the
simulator would leave OpenSimulator incompatible with the SecondLife
client.  Therefore, the simulator has to touch the IM service. (It also
allows us to offload the work of delivering one to one Instant messages off
to the regions instead of relying on a potentially highly overloaded
bottleneck central server)

Please review and respond here.
