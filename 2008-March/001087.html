Our current policy has been to have people post patches to Mantis, but
Mantis tends to grow much faster than any of us can keep up with, which
means a lot of these patches are getting lost.

How do people feel about changing the policy to attach patches to emails
here?  This is a pretty typical approach in other open projects, and I
think would get patches a lot more attention.

It would also let us discuss them, and help people modify them in ways
that better fit with the project.
