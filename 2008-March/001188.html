The idea of the project is to enable a Open Source standards-based system of voice over the internet for any and all virtual worlds.

We're starting out small, replacing the vivox component of the Second Life client viewer with something that can speak with a SIP-based backend, but we're hoping you can help us grow from there.

There is now a mailing list for discussions here:
