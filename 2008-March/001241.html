This is really a non-issue. As was pointed out earlier in this thread, words
cannot be trademarked; they're copyrighted. Only a specific instance of a
hippo may be trademarked; only a specific graphic representation of the
concept of 'grid' can be trademarked. Even domain names are safe unless they
incorporate the phrase 'Second Life' or 'Linden Labs'.

As we are not a corporate entity (at least, not yet), we have no particular
need of registering trademarks.
