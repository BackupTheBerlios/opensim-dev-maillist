For all of you folks who continue to argue the merits of including this bit
of code in core:

It is the overwhelming consensus that this is not the best course of action,
in spite of anyone's misunderstanding of my (or other's) comments.

For those who seem to think the design of Diva's code is up for discussion,
I assure you that that is at best highly unlikely, though I rather suspect
she is capable of taking her counsel where she chooses.

That being said, opensim-dev is not a proper venue to discuss the merits of
a given web interface, potential or otherwise. Please take this part of the
discussion off-list.
