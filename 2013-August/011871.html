I note several inconsistencies in the use of "-" in OpenSim.exe 
command console command lines.  I always seem to get this wrong.  and

create region ["region name"] <region_file.ini> - Create a new region.

delete-region <name> - Delete a region from disk

remove-region <name> - Remove a region from this simulator

Can this have a tidy up ahead of any major release to help 
people?  Perhaps the hyphen could even be optional where it makes 
sense so both formats work?

And we have verb subject in most cases... and it is what one might 
expect in general...

load iar
save iar

and yet we sometimes have the subject ahead of the verb as in

terrain load
terrain save

so its the opposite way round to what one might expect.
