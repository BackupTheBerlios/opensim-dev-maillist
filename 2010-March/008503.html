> Hi folks.  It's that time of year again when the clocks go forward for
> Summer Time in large parts of the Americas and Europe.
>
> As usual, this means that we will move the general Tuesday OpenSim
> development office hour (
> http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Office_hours#Tuesday_-_Office_Hour) from
> 1900 UTC to 1800 UTC from Tuesday 30th March 2010 onwards.
>
> For much of Europe this means that the office hour will continue to be at
> exactly the same time (since the clocks go forward for Daylight Savings Time
> on Sunday 28th March).
>
> For much of the Americas, this means that the office hour will be ONE HOUR
> EARLIER next week (Tuesday 23rd March) but will revert to the normal time on
> Tuesday 30th March onwards.
