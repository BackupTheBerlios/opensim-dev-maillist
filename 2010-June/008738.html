Hi everyone,

0.7-rc1 is out. You can grab it from the usual place:
http://dist.opensimulator.org/

Please read the release notes and upgrade guide before you upgrade:
http://opensimulator.org/wiki/0.7_Release

Also before you upgrade, make sure that you try it first on a test DB, 
or on a cloned DB. At the very least do a backup of your DB before you 
upgrade.

Never upgrade a production grid without testing things out on a beta 
grid first.

Issue reports welcome --> mantis.

Enjoy!
