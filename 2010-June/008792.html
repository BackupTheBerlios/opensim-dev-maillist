You need to clean up all OpenSim dlls and exes, as in nant clean or 
whatever is equivalent in Windows.
Usually, rm OpenSim.*.dll OpenSim.*.exe
Then re-build.
Remove Old Guids from the connection strings.

This is just for those wanting to help confirm that the old driver 
succeeds, while the new one fails. It would be good to that confirmation 
from several people.
