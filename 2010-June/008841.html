I only found out late last night, and I havent had a chance to file a mantis
yet, but the short story is, Prims with content in them, say a script for
instance, that references a Invalid AssetID will crash a save oar (
http://pastebin.com/6Xtjafn9 ).   what is nice though is the offending
scripts UUIDs are printed as you can see in the error, so what i did was go
into PrimItems table and erased everything that pointed to these invalid
AssetIDs and now OAR works again.  I will get a mantis filed shortly, Thanks
Justin.
