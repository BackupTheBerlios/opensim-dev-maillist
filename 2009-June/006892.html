Hi Ursula,
To my knowledge there have not been any changes to trunk in the last 2-3
weeks that would prevent sculpted prims from having a collision surface. If
these are not new sculpties, you may want to make sure that they still exist
on your asset server and that the asset server is working properly. Often
viewers will cache a sculpt map and display it even when the region cannot
access it. Also, you may want to check your OpenSim.log file and see if you
can spot any errors related to sculpted prims or asset fetch failures.

-dahlia
