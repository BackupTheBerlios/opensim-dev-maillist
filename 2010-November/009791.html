* For those of you following the master branch:

In order to preserve creator information on HG asset transfers, I just 
committed some changes that affect the HG configs quite substantially. 
Please make sure to look at the diffs of Robust.HG.ini.example (many 
changes), and config-include/StandaloneCommon.ini.example and 
config-include/GridCommon.ini.example (just minor changes). Non-HG 
configs aren't affected.

In a nutshell, I added a separate HGAssetService that is now the one 
that sticks its head as the interface to the outside world. For now, it 
just rewrites the creator information of objects that go out. In the 
near future it will perform authorization checks and all sorts of other 
filtering on inter-grid asset requests.

Bugs ==> mantis ==> thanks!

* For everyone else, all of this will be documented when the next 
release comes out.
