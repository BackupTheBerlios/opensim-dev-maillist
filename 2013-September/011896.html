Diva only ensures that Wifi works with OpenSim releases, and offers no 
support or updates to make it work with development code.

If you drop in her binary distribution for 0.7.5, and don't use her 
mysql connector, then it will work fine, you just won't have any updated 
numbers on your Wifi page. This is what I'm currently doing.

She will likely update Wifi for 0.7.6 once it has been released. Then 
we'll be able to use it until something changes in the 0.7.7 dev code to 
break it and we'll be S.O.L. again, until the next release. It's the way 
of things. :-)
