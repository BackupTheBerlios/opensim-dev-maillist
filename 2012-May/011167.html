I think just the usual kinds of tips that you've outlined apply.  If the use case allows it, you might want to consider 
implementing any time critical or complex functions in region modules instead.  Scripts can still communicate with these 
either via modSendCommand() (in OpenSimulator 0.7.3.1 and earlier) or custom functions added by the module (in current 
OpenSimulator development code).
