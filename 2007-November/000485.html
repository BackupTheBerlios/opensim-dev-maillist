I think this sounds quite interesting, although I feel the same as Adam, in that I would need to know more details. Can't really find out anything about realXtend or what work has been done on it already. 

So without knowing more details, it sounds like they have their own ideas, and so therefore want to have their own project (realXtend) rather than just joining us to work on opensim. So the most likely best situation is the sort of collaboration that 3di and TribalMedia already do with opensim.
