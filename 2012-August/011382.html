Hallo Gopi,

I am not quite sure what it is exactly you are asking. Grids do not have 
to be registered nor is there direct development support (aside from the 
mailinglist). All information about setting up a grid is available at 
http://www.opensimulator.org, where you can also find development 
information. If the infos there are not sufficient for you, you can ask 
here at the list.

If you need somebody to help you hands-on, you can ask here at the list, 
but most likely you will have to pay for it. There are also quite a few 
companies around who will provide the infrastructure and the 
installation for you (look at
http://www.hypergridbusiness.com/opensim-hosting-providers/)

Regards,

Klaus
