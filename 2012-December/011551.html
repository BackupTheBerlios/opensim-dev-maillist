Hi all,

let me firstly explain what i intend to do.

i am going to build an intelligent tutoring system that uses OpenSim to
teach the learner by:

1- asking the learner to perform a sequence of tasks.
2- the system will register all the activities and events that have been
performed by the learner (e,g, how many minutes he/she spent to perform (x)
task, did he/she cooperate with other learners on the time of performing
(x) task, what are the locations he/she stay in and what is his/her best
classmate ;) etc...)
3- the system should store all of these data in DB or XML:file to build a
learner model.-
4- then, if the learner do need a help the system can detect that then the
system will intelligently guide the learner based on his/her contributions
that have been stored in the learner model (e.g. the system will ask the
learner to perform an addition task with his/her classmate on the way they
usually do or the system give the learner a further task to do in his/her
desired location he/she loves to stay in.

i hope this detail give you good view what is the type of the events i
would like to capture.
