At 8:57 PM -0700 3/12/13, Mic Bowman wrote:

>maybe a better question... are inventory types 
>used for anything other than to put
>the right icon in the viewer (without the need 
>to pull the asset) and put stuff in the
>right default folder?


According to http://opensimulator.org/wiki/Custom_Libraries

« Inventory type is what tells the viewer which 
sort of icon to show next to the inventory item's 
name. »


It also controls the variable part of the 
contextual menu ("Open", "Wear", "Attach", 
"Play", "Teleport") and which editor to use.
