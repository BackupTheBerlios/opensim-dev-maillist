On MediaWiki, you can tell reCaptcha to show the captcha when links are
being posted.  In fact, I beleive it is the default behavior.  If you're
getting a ton of spam and have few volunteers, here's a few suggestions:

The following will force all groups, except for sysops and bots, to show the
captcha:

$wgGroupPermissions['user'         ]['skipcaptcha'] =
false;$wgGroupPermissions['autoconfirmed']['skipcaptcha'] = false;

If you want to manually confirm users, make a new group and add it to
skipcaptcha.

In addition, adding a recent-changes channel for the wiki allows
vandal-fighting tools like VandalFighter to connect and show changes in
real-time to your volunteers, in addition to volunteers themselves.

Installing patrolling extensions can also help.

Adding a blacklisting extension can allow you to automatically block people
who post certain patterns.

Hope this helps.
