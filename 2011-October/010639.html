Fritigern, I think the difference is Wikipedia has literally 100's if not
1000's of volunteers, try posting something on Wikipedia, it will literally
be erased within minutes if not seconds of you posting it.  I am not saying
we should not install these things, but I can tell you in the example of the
OSgrid wiki, everything you mentioned did absolutely nothing to stop spam,
in fact I can pretty much say that our spam even increased over time after
having them installed, I even shut off log ins for about 2 months, and then
decided maybe enough time had passed and that the spammers would move on,
and literally within 5 minutes of turning log ins back on, the spam started
up again, whatever is doing it is fully automated, and captcha/recaptcha had
literally no effect.
