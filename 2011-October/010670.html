i agree with Chris' approach - it's no different than the 'no 
facelights' option.  they bother some common users but are quite useful 
in machinima and virtual photography.  it's the responsibility of the 
grid operator(s) to enable/disable popular features.

also, not everyone is confused or frustrated by display names and not 
everyone abuses them.  they definitely have a function and a place, even 
in OpenSim (eventually).  keep in mind, we're using SL as a reference 
point here... the rest of that thought should be self-explanatory ;)

more valuable than a personal opinion about a feature would be some sort 
of documentation illustrating any caveats users/administrators should be 
made aware of in the interest of community education.
