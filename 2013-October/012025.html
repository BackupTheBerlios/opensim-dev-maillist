Thanks Mark.  I believe the problem is being triggered by the fact that you do not have a

Cap_UploadBakedTexture = localhost

entry in your [ClientStack.LindenCaps] section.  This means that the viewers are falling back to the older baked texture 
upload mechanism through the ASSET XFER UPLOADER as I can see now in the log.  But for some reason this is not saving 
the asset, so the appearance checker cannot find it and requests a rebake, ad infinitum.

These really should be loaded in from your OpenSimDefaults.ini file so I'm quite puzzled as to why they aren't there.  I 
would suggest checking this file and replacing it with a fresh one from the OpenSimulator 0.7.6 package if necessary.
