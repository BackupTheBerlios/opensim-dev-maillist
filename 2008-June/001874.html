LSL: Linden Scripting Language
EBNF: Extended Backus-Naur Form

To my understanding, I think it should be "ABNF" -- Augmented
Backus-Naur Form -- and its definition would be in RFC2234 (available
from rfc-editor.org).  It has historically been used in defining the
Internet protocol standards.

I don't know of any definition of LSL in ABNF.
