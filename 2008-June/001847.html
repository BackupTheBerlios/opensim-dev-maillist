Tomorrow the AWG Groupies will be discussing how the interoperable login
and teleport protocols should relate region handles. The AWG Groupies
meetings are open; you do not have to be a group member to participate.
In fact the attendee list generally includes a wide variety of non-AWG
second life residents, Lindens such as myself, and even the occasional
press avatar.

I'm writing this because I think Opensim developers would have useful
input on this topic, should any be able to attend with such short
notice. The meeting is in Second Life, in the center of the
ThorneBridgeTown region, from 9:30am to 11:00am PDT.
