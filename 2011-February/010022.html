Just an update to the problem.

1.       In a 2X2 estate (4 regions) there are no problems regarding missing
physical objects.

2.       In a 3X3 estate, 2 regions can't keep any physical objects.

3.       In a 4X4 estate, 6 regions can't keep any physical objects.

4.       In any case, in the region right to the SW one, some physical
objects always get below ground after a restart (this also happens to some
other regions depending on the size of the estate).

5.       When shutting down, some physical objects get negative group
position in the z axis. Manual correction of this issue directly to the
database records, doesn't prevent the disappearance of them.

6.       In the server console I get endless messages regarding storing of
the physical objects. Are they constantly changing position and need to be
stored again and again?

7.       Diva as well as Opensim (latest versions both), behave the same.
So, I guess it's not a database issue (MySQL or SQLite).

8.       I cannot test if Opensim.exe behaves the same as
OpenSim.32BitLaunch.exe, as Justin suggested (all my installations are Win7
64bit).

 

As mentioned in my previous post, suggestions and ideas are most welcome.
