think this is one of those things that really needs a proper spec 
formulated before implementation.  A security infrastructure (as we've 
already seen) affects a lot of things and can impact many use cases.

don't go with something that has holes that will be difficult to put 
right later.

It's very difficult to determine the arguments via multiple stream of 
consciousness e-mails, especially if they're not written in a 
developer's first language.  This isn't meant as an insult but merely as 
a statement of fact.
