OpenSimForge is here - this idea was cooked up by a few of the regular committers, and we've setup a separate dedicated server for it.

URL: http://forge.opensimulator.org

There's a number of opensim-related projects out there at the moment, such as the ASP.NET gridservers we developed, the Perl ones 3Di did, etc. With this, you can register a project for each of those and get some space for the hosting of it (includes mailing lists, forums, trackers, svn/cvs, etc)

What it's for:

-          Alternate Gridserver Implementations

-          OpenSim Region Modules

-          OpenSim Plugins

-          OpenSim-derived Applications

-          Unofficial OpenSim branches (ie interop patches, etc)

What requirements exist for committing here?

-          Must be OSS under a OSI-approved license, or public domain. BSD-licensed is preferred but not required.

-          Must have some code already (ie, don't apply for a project space until you have at least a few lines of code, even in an alpha state)

How do I apply for space?

-          Register an account and activate it

-          In your control panel ("My Stuff") hit "Register new project"

-          Fill out the details

-          Wait for a admin to approve the project (all the opensim committers will have accounts capable of doing this)


Enjoy.

Adam
