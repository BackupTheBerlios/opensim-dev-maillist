We are a new-born group of Italian people enthusiast about OpenSim and
willing to help and improve OpenSim.

Currently around 15 people are actively synchronizing

* using an Italian skype chat group,
* organizing Italian OpenSim Meetings in SecondLife
* trying to setup an Italian wiki website to help Italian opensim community.

Among us there are programmers but also simple users willing to help in
coordinating, documenting, testing and participating.

Programmers (Vytek, Junta, Salahzar) are already actively helping in solving
some Mantis issues, and we provided some patches in latest weeks inserted in
the svn trunk, but we feel that some help and guidance is needed, especially
to understand how to solve problems in an effective and not too naive way.
Compatibly with our status of volunteers with limited time and resources. :)

JustinCC started to produce some documentation from his blog, but it is
still greatly incomplete.
Thanks for any advice and direct contact & help :)

Salahzar Stenvaag for the OpenSimIT group
