Hi Christophe and all,

I am also interested in this useful feature and experimented a
bit. I searched for the position in the OpenSim-Code, where the login and
the logout process (the first and the last client-server messages) takes
place. There I implemented an add-/ delete-method, which handles the
login/logout for users with "guest" as last name. The method adds/ deletes
this users to/from the userserver database.
The limitation of the guest users permissions is certainly still open. My
first idea was to achive this issue per group policy (through a
guest-group), but in my view, the group module in opensim is too
undeveloped, just now.
I would be grateful for collaboration and suggestions on how to solve this
issue.
