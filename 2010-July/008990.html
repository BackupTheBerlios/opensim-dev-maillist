I would like to add that I can log in to the current MOAP with Simian 
Frontend release (13-Jul-2010 13260) with the Second Life 2 Beta Test 
latest viewer... Second Life 2.1.0 (206543) too.

I note just three things that are not quite right with this setup and 
viewer which I am sure that Justin will be aware of, but just so he 
is aware of the tests to date...

a) the prim with media on a face needs to have one click on it before 
the media/web page shows even though  auto play of media is set to 
true in preferences,.

b) my avatar body parts and clothing does not survive a relog, though 
I can wear it again straight away when I come back in by manually 
dropping the folder onto my avatar or selecting to replace or add to 
my appearance.

c) before Enrico used the Simian front end, the Second Life Viewer 2 
Beta showed the media on a prim, but the avatar (and others in world 
nearby) always appeared as a cloud.

Cheers, thanks to you, and a VERY BIG thanks to Justin for stating to 
look at MOAP in Opensim, which I think is an important development.
