I have been working on a Bullet based physics engine for OpenSimulator.  It is in a fairly stable state and works pretty well as a regular physics engine.

If you have the time and resources, I invite testing and stressing. The version checked into 'master' sources works on Windows and Linux both 32 and 64 bit. BulletSim is turned on my changing the physics engine selection in "OpenSim.ini" - change "physics=OpenDynamicsEngine" to "physics=BulletSim". That's it!
