As a special added note too...

There are viewers out there now that will allow you to copy ANYTHING out of
Second Life - even attachments while they are being worn by other avatars.
However, Linden Lab is very familiar with these viewers and they check
frequently for them and will automatically and instantly permanently ban you
and will refuse to negotiate for reinstatement.

So please be very cautious if you are considering using hacked viewer that
will let you copy things out of Second Life.  If you're banned, then
obviously you have permanently lost access to all of the items in your SL
inventory.

Just a friendly word of caution.

I have one of these viewers and use it within my home opensim server as an
additional means of keeping a backup of my buildings, etc.  I have several
thousand dollars invested in Second Life so I don't dare try using the
viewer there.  but I personally know people who have used them and got
banned.  Nastiness all around.
